🚀 What is the Sound Barrier?

The "sound barrier" is what happens when something flies as fast as sound - that's called Mach 1!

💡
Big Idea: When you fly as fast as sound, AIR PILES UP in front of you! It's like pushing through an invisible wall!
🐌

Subsonic

SLOWER than sound - air moves aside smoothly

⚡

Transonic

AT speed of sound - air starts piling up!

🚀

Supersonic

FASTER than sound - you broke through!

💥

The Boom!

Breaking through makes a SONIC BOOM!

📊 Understanding Mach Numbers

Mach number tells us how fast something is compared to the speed of sound.

Mach 0.5 Half the speed of sound
Mach 1.0 THE SOUND BARRIER! 767 mph
Mach 2.0 Twice the speed of sound!
Mach 3.0+ SUPER FAST! Fighter jets
