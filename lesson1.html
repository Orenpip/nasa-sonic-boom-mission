What is Sound?

Learn how vibrations make sound!

🎵 Sound is Everywhere!

Sound is all around us! Music, talking, birds singing, cars beeping - it's all sound!

But what IS sound? Sound happens when something vibrates.

💡
Big Idea: When things vibrate (shake back and forth), they make sound!
🎸

Guitar String

Pluck a guitar string. It vibrates and makes sound!

🥁

Drum

Hit a drum. The drum head vibrates and makes sound!

🔔

Bell

Ring a bell. The metal vibrates and makes sound!

👄

Your Voice

When you talk, parts in your throat vibrate!

🌊 Sound Makes Waves

When something vibrates, it pushes the air around it.

The air moves in waves - like waves in water!

These sound waves travel through the air to your ears. That's how you hear things!
