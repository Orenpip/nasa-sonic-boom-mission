What is Sound?

Discover vibrations, waves, and how we hear!

🎵 Sound is Everywhere!

Music, talking, birds singing, cars honking - it's all SOUND!

💡
Big Idea: Sound happens when something VIBRATES (shakes back and forth really fast)!
🎸

Guitar String

Pluck it - it vibrates and makes sound!

🥁

Drum

Hit it - the drum head vibrates!

🔔

Bell

Ring it - metal vibrates and rings!

👄

Your Voice

Talk - your throat vibrates!

🌊 Sound Makes Waves!

When something vibrates, it pushes and pulls the air around it. This creates sound waves that travel through the air!

➡️

Compression

Air molecules pushed TOGETHER

⬅️

Rarefaction

Air molecules pulled APART

👆 This is what a sound wave looks like!
