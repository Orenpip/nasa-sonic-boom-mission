Loud & Quiet Sounds

Discover volume and amplitude!

🔊 What is Volume?

Volume is how LOUD or QUIET a sound is. A whisper is QUIET. A fire truck siren is LOUD!

💡
Big Idea: BIG vibrations (big waves) make LOUD sounds. Small vibrations (small waves) make QUIET sounds!
📢

Loud Sounds

Sirens, thunder, shouting!

🤫

Quiet Sounds

Whispers, leaves rustling!

👂

Your Ears

Very loud sounds can hurt!

📏

Distance

Sounds get quieter far away!

📊 Measuring Loudness: Decibels (dB)

🔬 Cool Volume Facts!

1

Protect Your Ears

Sounds over 85 dB can damage your hearing if you listen for too long!

2

Blue Whale

Blue whales make the loudest sound of any animal - 188 dB! That's louder than a jet engine!

3

Distance Matters

Every time you double the distance from a sound, it gets about 6 dB quieter!

4

Library Rule

Libraries try to keep noise under 40 dB - that's quieter than normal talking!
