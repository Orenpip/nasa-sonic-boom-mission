High & Low Sounds

Discover pitch and frequency!

🎵 What is Pitch?

Pitch is how HIGH or LOW a sound is. A whistle is HIGH pitch. Thunder is LOW pitch!

💡
Big Idea: FAST vibrations make HIGH sounds. SLOW vibrations make LOW sounds!
🐦

High Pitch

Birds, whistles, squeaky toys!

🥁

Low Pitch

Drums, thunder, big bells!

🎹

Piano Keys

Left = LOW, Right = HIGH!

👶

Voices

Kids = higher than adults!

⚡ Frequency: Counting Vibrations

Frequency means how many times something vibrates in one second. We measure it in Hertz (Hz).

🎯 Cool Pitch Facts!

1

Tiny Voices

Kids have higher voices because their vocal cords are smaller and vibrate faster!

2

Dog Whistles

Some whistles are SO high-pitched that only dogs can hear them!

3

Musical Instruments

Big instruments make low sounds. Small instruments make high sounds!

4

Hearing Range

Humans can hear from 20 Hz to 20,000 Hz - that's a LOT of different pitches!
