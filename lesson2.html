How Sound Travels

Discover how sound moves through different materials!

🌊 Sound Needs Something to Travel Through

Sound can't travel through empty space! It needs a medium - something for the vibrations to move through.

💡
Big Idea: Sound travels through AIR, WATER, and SOLID objects. But it travels at different speeds through each one!
💨

Through Air

We hear most sounds through air!

🌊

Through Water

Whales "talk" underwater!

🪵

Through Solids

Sound travels through walls and doors!

🚫

Not Through Space

No sound in outer space - no air!

🔬 Cool Sound Travel Facts!

1

Whales Talk Far Away

Whales can communicate hundreds of miles underwater because sound travels so well in water!

2

Knock on Wood

Put your ear on a table and tap it - you'll hear it super loud because sound travels fast through wood!

3

Silent Space

In space movies, explosions make noise - but real space is totally silent!

4

Train Tracks

People used to put their ear on train tracks to hear trains coming from far away!
