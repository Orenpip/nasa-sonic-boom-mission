💥 What is a Sonic Boom?

A sonic boom is the LOUD sound you hear when something flies FASTER than the speed of sound!

💡
Big Idea: When you go faster than sound, you create a SHOCKWAVE! That shockwave is the sonic boom you hear on the ground!

🔬 How Sonic Booms Work

When something flies slower than sound, sound waves move ahead of it. But when it goes FASTER than sound, it catches up to its own sound waves!

🎯 Amazing Sonic Boom Facts!

💥

Double Boom

You actually hear TWO booms - one from the nose and one from the tail of the plane!

📏

The Faster, The Louder

The faster the plane goes, the LOUDER the sonic boom! Mach 3 makes a bigger boom than Mach 1.5.

🏠

Shake Buildings

Sonic booms are so powerful they can rattle windows and shake buildings!
