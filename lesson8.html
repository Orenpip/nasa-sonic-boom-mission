🏆 What Makes a Good Design?

🎯

Pointy Nose

Sharp noses cut through air better (less air resistance!)

⚖️

Good Balance

Weight in the front helps it fly straight

✈️

Even Wings

Both wings should be exactly the same size

📐

Straight Folds

Neat, straight folds = better flight!
